﻿About﻿

Thanks for visiting my site. I am an artist based in Wellington, Aotearoa / New Zealand.

I like to express my self creatively through art forms such as coding, drawing, jewellery, tattooing and sewing.
I am currently studying web development and am interested in algorithmic & generative art. I have been experimenting with technologies such as processing and three.js

A lot of my work is inspired by natural forms like landscapes, organisms, fluid dynamics, microscopy and crystalline structures. But also archaeology, magical/mystical traditions, outsider art and the Art Nouveau/Jugendstil movements.

I am mostly self taught..

I also like to garden :)

 - Jess Stuart
